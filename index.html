The Story

A journey that started with one workout and a decision to keep showing up.

Rajiv's journey into health and wellness didn't start in a gym or a classroom. It started with an invite from a friend and a decision to show up, even when it was hard. After more than a decade in the culinary industry, rising to Sous Chef at one of the Pacific Northwest's premier catering companies, that one workout changed everything.

He kept showing up. Day after day, something began to shift, not just in his body, but in how he showed up in every area of his life. His relationships. His community. His sense of purpose.

"That transformation lit a fire that has never gone out."

He went on to pursue a career in health and wellness, spending over a decade helping individuals build the habits, discipline, and life rhythms that create lasting change. That journey led him here, to leaders. Because the people who carry the most are often the ones being carried the least.
